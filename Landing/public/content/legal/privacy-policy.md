# Privacy Policy

*Last updated: November 5, 2024*

**We will never sell your data. Period.**

This policy explains what data we collect, why, and how we protect it.

## What We Collect

### Account Information
- Name and email address (to create and manage your account)
- Password (encrypted)

### Payment Information
- Payment details (processed by third-party processor - we never see full card numbers)
- Billing address (for taxes and fraud prevention)
- Transaction history (last 4 digits, dates, amounts)

### Usage Data
- IP address (for security and fraud prevention)
- Browser and device info (to optimize the Service)
- Activity logs (which features you use, when you access the Service)
- Error logs (to fix bugs and improve performance)

### Cookies & Tracking
We use cookies for:
- Keeping you logged in
- Analytics (via third-party services like Google Analytics)
- Advertising effectiveness measurement

You can control cookies in your browser settings, but some features may not work without them.

## How We Use Your Data

- Provide and improve the Service
- Process payments and prevent fraud
- Send account updates and security alerts
- Provide customer support
- Analyze usage to improve features
- Comply with legal obligations

## When We Share Your Data

We only share your data in these situations:

**Service Providers**: Third parties that help us operate (hosting, payments, analytics, email). They can only use your data for their specific function.

**Legal Requirements**: When required by valid legal process (subpoenas, court orders). We'll notify you unless prohibited by law.

**Business Transfers**: If we're acquired or merged, your data transfers to the new owner. We'll notify you first.

**With Your Consent**: Any other sharing requires your explicit permission.

## Your Rights

- **Access**: View your data in account settings
- **Correction**: Update your information anytime
- **Deletion**: Request account deletion at info@chat2deal.com
- **Export**: Request data export at info@chat2deal.com
- **Opt-out**: Unsubscribe from emails using the link in any message

## Data Security

We protect your data with:
- SSL/TLS encryption for all transmissions
- Encrypted password storage
- Limited employee access
- Regular security audits

No system is 100% secure, but we work hard to protect your information.

## Location

Our servers are in the United States. By using chat2deal, you consent to data transfer and storage in the US.

## Children

chat2deal is not for users under 13. We don't knowingly collect data from children.

## Changes

We may update this policy. When we make significant changes, we'll notify you via email and update the date above.
