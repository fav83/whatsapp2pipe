# How to Capture WhatsApp Leads into Pipedrive Instantly (No Copy-Paste)

You just had a great conversation with a potential customer on WhatsApp. They're interested, they've asked the right questions, and you know this could turn into a deal. Now comes the part every sales rep dreads: switching tabs, opening Pipedrive, manually typing in their name and phone number, and hoping you spelled everything correctly.

By the time you're done, another message pops up. And another. The momentum is gone, and that lead you were so excited about? It sits in your CRM with a typo in the name—or worse, it never makes it there at all.

If you're using WhatsApp Web for sales conversations and Pipedrive as your CRM, this friction is costing you deals. The good news is there's a better way. Chat2Deal is a Chrome extension that connects WhatsApp Web directly to Pipedrive, letting you capture leads without ever leaving your conversation.

## The Problem: Manual Data Entry Kills Sales Momentum

Let's be honest about what happens when leads live in WhatsApp but your CRM is in another tab:

**Lost context.** You're mid-conversation, the prospect is warm, and you need to log their details. By the time you've switched tabs, found the right form, and entered the information, you've lost the thread of the conversation.

**No visibility for managers.** If it's not in the CRM, it didn't happen. Research suggests that a significant portion of WhatsApp conversations never get logged—which means pipeline reports are incomplete, forecasts are inaccurate, and opportunities slip through the cracks.

**Slow follow-up.** When reps have to context-switch between WhatsApp and Pipedrive, response times suffer. That quick "let me check and get back to you" turns into hours because logging the contact took priority over responding.

**Information hoarding.** When a rep leaves, their WhatsApp conversations walk out the door with them. Without those contacts in your CRM, you've lost not just the leads but all the context around them.

The solution isn't to work harder at manual data entry. It's to eliminate it entirely.

## What Chat2Deal Does (30-Second Overview)

Chat2Deal is a Chrome extension that adds a sidebar to WhatsApp Web. When you click on any 1:1 conversation, it automatically looks up that phone number in your Pipedrive account.

- **Contact exists?** You see their Pipedrive profile right in the sidebar, complete with a link to open them in Pipedrive.
- **Contact doesn't exist?** You can create them in one click, with the name and phone number pre-filled from WhatsApp.
- **Contact exists but with a different number?** You can attach this WhatsApp number to their existing profile.

No copy-paste. No tab switching. No manual data entry. The sidebar lives right where you work, so capturing a lead takes seconds, not minutes.

## Step-by-Step Setup (60 Seconds to Your First Lead)

Getting started with Chat2Deal takes about a minute:

### Step 1: Install the Extension

Visit the Chrome Web Store and search for "Chat2Deal" or go directly to the extension page. Click **Add to Chrome**, then confirm the installation.

The extension requires minimal permissions—just access to WhatsApp Web so it can display the sidebar where you work.

### Step 2: Open WhatsApp Web

Navigate to web.whatsapp.com in Chrome. Once WhatsApp Web loads, you'll notice the Chat2Deal sidebar appear on the right side of the screen.

### Step 3: Sign In with Pipedrive

Click **Sign in with Pipedrive** in the sidebar. This opens a secure OAuth window where you authorize Chat2Deal to access your Pipedrive account.

Chat2Deal uses Pipedrive's official OAuth flow—your credentials are never stored by the extension. Once you authorize access, the sidebar is ready to use.

### Step 4: Click on a Conversation

That's it. Click on any 1:1 WhatsApp conversation, and Chat2Deal automatically looks up that contact in Pipedrive. The whole setup takes about 60 seconds.

## Creating Your First Contact from WhatsApp

Let's walk through capturing a new lead:

**1. Start a WhatsApp conversation**

You receive a message from a new prospect. Their number shows up in WhatsApp, but they're not in your CRM yet.

**2. Chat2Deal shows "No match found"**

The sidebar automatically searches Pipedrive for their phone number. When it doesn't find a match, you'll see two options: **Create Person** and **Attach to Existing**.

**3. Click Create Person**

The creation form appears with the contact's name pre-filled from their WhatsApp display name. Their phone number is already there—you just need to review and click Save.

**4. Contact saved to Pipedrive**

The new person appears in Pipedrive with their WhatsApp phone number saved under a "WhatsApp" label. The sidebar now shows their profile card with a link to open them directly in Pipedrive.

The entire process takes under 10 seconds. No switching tabs, no copying phone numbers, no typos.

### What Gets Saved

When you create a contact through Chat2Deal:

- **Name:** Pre-filled from WhatsApp's display name (you can edit before saving)
- **Phone:** The contact's phone number, labeled as "WhatsApp"
- **Email:** Optional field you can fill in if known

Chat2Deal doesn't access or store message content—only the phone number and display name needed to create or match contacts.

## Attaching a Phone Number to an Existing Pipedrive Person

Sometimes you already have a contact in Pipedrive, but they're messaging you from a different number—maybe their personal WhatsApp instead of their work phone.

**1. Click on the conversation**

Chat2Deal searches for the phone number and doesn't find a match.

**2. Click "Attach to Existing"**

Instead of creating a duplicate contact, you can search your Pipedrive contacts by name.

**3. Search and select**

Type the person's name to search your Pipedrive database. When you find the right contact, select them.

**4. Phone number attached**

Their WhatsApp number is added to their existing Pipedrive profile as an additional phone with the "WhatsApp" label. Future conversations from this number will automatically match to their profile.

This prevents duplicate contacts while ensuring you capture every channel through which a prospect reaches you.

## Bonus: View and Manage Deals Without Leaving WhatsApp

Once a contact exists in Pipedrive, Chat2Deal shows more than just their profile. You can also see and manage their deals directly from the sidebar.

**View all associated deals**

For any matched contact, the sidebar displays their deals—open, won, and lost—with status indicators and current values.

**Create new deals**

When a conversation turns into an opportunity, create a deal right from the sidebar. Select a pipeline and stage, optionally add a value, and save. The deal is linked to the contact automatically.

**Update deal stages**

As conversations progress, move deals through your pipeline without leaving WhatsApp. Change the stage from a dropdown and save—the update syncs to Pipedrive instantly.

**Close deals**

Mark deals as won or lost directly from the sidebar. For lost deals, you can add an optional reason to track why opportunities didn't close.

This means your entire workflow—from lead capture to deal closure—can happen without switching away from your WhatsApp conversations. We'll cover deal management in depth in a [separate guide on managing Pipedrive deals from WhatsApp](/blog/manage-pipedrive-deals-whatsapp).

## What Chat2Deal Doesn't Do

To set clear expectations:

- **No message sending:** Chat2Deal helps you capture and organize contacts—it doesn't send messages on your behalf
- **No group chat support:** The extension works with 1:1 conversations only
- **No message content storage:** Chat2Deal never reads or stores your WhatsApp messages (except when you explicitly save selected messages as notes)
- **Pipedrive only:** Currently designed specifically for Pipedrive CRM users
